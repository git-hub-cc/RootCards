词根（可能无）

单词1 **bury** 埋葬
例句1 He was **buried** in the churchyard.
例句2 She **buried** herself in her work.
补充笔记1：B字母表示鼓出来，突出来，埋葬后会堆出一个小土丘，所以埋葬和鼓出来有关联。引申意：埋葬在工作中，即埋头于工作。固定搭配：**bury oneself in doing something**。

单词2 **calculate** 计算
例句 He couldn't **calculate** the distance.
补充笔记1：词根**calc**表示石头，以前人类用小石头计算，所以 calculate 是计算。名词：**calculator**。

单词3 **cancel** 取消
例句 I’m afraid I’ll have to **cancel** our meeting tomorrow.
补充笔记1：动词：**cancel something**，**something be canceled**。

单词4 **candidate** 候选人
例句 There are only six **candidates** for the job.
补充笔记1：ate是后辍，did表示做，能够做事的，即候选人。引申意：申请求职者，考生。

单词5 **carbon** 碳
例句 **Carbon dioxide** is part of the economy.
补充笔记1：carbon dioxide是二氧化碳，di是前缀，表示二，oxide是氧化物，氧。

单词6 **celebrate** 庆祝
例句1 We had a tea party to **celebrate** our parents’ silver wedding.
补充笔记1：celebrate和celebrity（名人）同源，名人所到之处，人们都在庆祝。

单词7 **ceremony** 庆典仪式
例句 Did you watch the opening **ceremony** of the Olympic games?
补充笔记1：celebrate和ceremony同源，慶典儀式即庆祝很多（**many**）东西，人类最早庆祝丰收。

单词8 **chaos** 混乱
例句 The room is in **chaos**.
补充笔记1：**be in chaos**：处于混乱中。Chaos是希腊神话中的混沌之神。

单词9 **cheat** 欺骗
例句1 She **cheats** on her husband.
例句2 I suspect he **cheated** in the exam.
补充笔记1：作弊：**cheat in the exam**。欺骗：**cheat on somebody**，专指婚姻或情感上的不忠。

单词10 **chest** 胸部
例句 His heart was pounding in his **chest**.
补充笔记1：胸部是一个区域，而**breast**（乳房）指的是特定部位。

单词11 **climate** 气候
例句 Dragon fruit does not grow in cold **climates**.
补充笔记1：**weather** 是指天气。

单词12 **cover** 覆盖
例句1 Ben **covered** his face with his hands.
例句2 The body was **discovered** in a field.
补充笔记1：C字母是核心，表示包围或半包围，即覆盖。引申意：封面，掩护。动词和名词都是**cover**。

单词13 **discover** 发现
例句1 The body was **discovered** in a field.
例句2 We made an important **discovery**.
补充笔记1：**dis** 是否定前缀，不覆盖，即发现。名词：**discovery**。

单词14 **recovery** 恢复
例句1 He’s in hospital, **recovering** from a heart attack.
例句2 He has made a full **recovery**.
补充笔记1：**re** 是再次，**cover** 是覆盖，即健康状态再次覆盖回来，所以是恢复。动词：**recover**。

单词15 **create** 创造
例句1 The new factory is expected to **create** more than 400 new jobs.
例句2 People believe that every living **creature** possesses a spirit.
补充笔记1：创造：**create**。名词：**creation**，**creature**（生物，所有上帝创造的东西）。

单词16 **recreate** 消遣娱乐
例句1 They **recreate** themselves with playing cards.
例句2 His only **recreations** are drinking beer and watching football.
补充笔记1：**re** 是再次，**create** 是创造，在经济学上，消遣娱乐消费也是在创造财富（GDP）。名词：**recreation**。

单词17 **concrete** 混凝土
例句1 The **concrete** will set after a certain time.
例句2 A dialogue about **concrete** issues and problems.
补充笔记1：**con** 是全部共同一起，**crete** 是创造，所有人创造出来的东西（石子，沙子，水泥的混合），即混凝土。引申意：具体的，实在的，可以摸得着的东西。

单词18 **crime** 犯罪
例句 We moved here because there was very little **crime**.
补充笔记1：犯罪：**crime**，罪犯：**criminal**。

单词19 **discriminate** 歧视
例句1 These policies **discriminate** against women.
例句2 **Racial discrimination** is not tolerated.
补充笔记1：**dis** 是分开，歧视是区分开。名词：**discrimination**。

单词20 **custom** 风俗习惯
例句1 That **custom** is very old.
例句2 She has formed the habit of drinking.
补充笔记1：**habit** 是个人习惯，**custom** 是所有人（一个地区，一个民族）的习惯。**customs** 加s是海关。

单词21 **accustomed** 习惯于
例句1 It took a while for me to **accustom** myself to all the new rules.
例句2 We were **accustomed** to working together.
补充笔记1：**ac** 是朝向，去，**custom** 是风俗习俗，即去习惯风俗习俗。动词：**accustom**。形容词：**accustomed**，**be accustomed to doing something**。

单词22 **dial** 拨打电话
例句 The number you **dialed** is not in service.
补充笔记1：拨打电话，常用词。

单词23 **develop** 发展
例句1 The new factory is expected to **develop** more than 400 new jobs.
例句2 People believe that every living **creature** possesses a spirit.
补充笔记3：发展，是不断扩大的画面感。引申意：养成（习惯），冲洗（照片）。

单词24 **envelope** 信封
例句 She recognized her mother’s handwriting on the **envelope**.
补充笔记1：**en** 是在里面，**lop** 是包，即在里面可以放东西的包。

单词25 **devote** 奉献
例句1 She **devoted** herself to caring for the sick.
例句2 His **devotion** to his children is amazing.
补充笔记1：**de** 是加強，**vote** 是发誓，即加强誓言，像宣誓一样奉献。搭配：**devote oneself to something**。名词：**devotion**。

单词26 **apologize** 道歉
例句1 You must **apologize** to your teacher.
例句2 She received an **apology** from the company.
补充笔记1：**ap** 是向下，**log** 是说，向下说是道歉。动词：**apologize to somebody**。名词：**apology**，**make an apology to somebody**。

单词27 **clumsy** 笨拙的
例句 A **clumsy** waitress spilled her coffee all over her new shirt.
补充笔记1：可联想**climb**（爬），爬楼梯或爬山时姿态笨拙。

单词28 **combine** 结合
例句1 **Combine** all the ingredients in a large bowl.
例句2 A **combination** of factors caused the accident.
补充笔记1：**com** 是共同一起，**bine** 是包，即全部包在一起。名词：**combination**。

单词29 **commerce** 商业
例句 She knows a lot about European **commerce**.
补充笔记1：**com** 是共同一起，商业即很多人聚集在一起。形容词：**commercial**。

单词30 **compulsory** 强制的
例句1 Car insurance is **compulsory**.
例句2 **Compulsory** education lasts for nine years.
补充笔记1：**com** 是共同一起，**puls** 是推拉，即所有人推拉着做一件事，是强制的。名词：**compulsion**。

单词31 **conflict** 冲突
例句 Peter is in **conflict** with his colleague.
补充笔记1：**con** 是共同一起，**flict** 是打，即共同打，冲突。

单词32 **continue** 继续
例句1 We can’t **continue** with the discussion until we have a proper agenda.
例句2 The whole **continent** of Africa.
补充笔记1：**con** 是共同一起，**tin** 是伸展，即一直延伸下去。

单词33 **continent** 大陆
例句1 We can’t **continue** with the discussion until we have a proper agenda.
例句2 The whole **continent** of Africa.
补充笔记1：**con** 是共同一起，**tin** 是伸展，指绵延无尽的大陆。

单词34 **cooperation** 合作
例句 The scheme works in **cooperation** with local businesses.
补充笔记1：**cooper** 是身体，即像人身体一样合作。

单词35 **cotton** 棉花
例句 A white shirt made from **cotton**.
补充笔记1：**cot** 是棉花，**cot** 是床，联想棉花是床单原材料。

单词36 **crew** 全体员工
例句 The plane crashed, killing two of the **crew** and four passengers.
补充笔记1：**staff** 是所有单位的全体员工，**crew** 仅指轮船、飞机、列车、剧组的全体员工。

单词37 **criterion** 标准
例句 Academic ability is not the sole **criterion** for admission to the college.
补充笔记1：单数：**criterion**，复数：**criteria**。

单词38 **die** 死亡
例句1 She is **dying** for food.
例句2 He is **dying** to eat meat.
补充笔记1：**die** 是死亡，**dying** 是垂死。**be dying to do something** 或 **be dying for something** 极其渴望。

单词39 **divide** 分开
例句 A good book **divides** people.
补充笔记1：**di** 是分开，即分开。名词：**division**。

单词40 **draw** 拉
例句1 **Draw** a picture of your house.
例句2 **Draw** the curtains, please.
例句3 My children love to **draw** and paint.
补充笔记1：**draw** 和 **drag**（拉）同源，指拉笔作画。**drawer**（抽屉）。

单词41 **draft** 草案
例句 The final **draft** of the speech.
补充笔记1：**draw** 和 **draft** 同源，指拉笔写出的初稿、草案。

单词42 **drawback** 缺点
例句 It’s a great city. The only **drawback** is the weather.
补充笔记1：**draw** 是拉，**back** 是后，即拉回到后面。同义词：**shortcoming**。

单词43 **withdraw** 撤退
例句1 People **withdraw** money from ATM.
例句2 **Withdraw** money from the account.
例句3 **Withdraw** an offer.
例句4 **Withdraw** a statement.
补充笔记1：**with** 是向后，**draw** 是拉，即向后拉。指撤退，取回（钱），取消，撤回（陈述）。名词：**withdrawal**。
