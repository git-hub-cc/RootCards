---
**词根: with- (向后)**
**withhold** 拒绝给予，保留
**例句1:** It might be necessary for us to withhold some key information.
我们可能有必要保留一些关键信息。
**withdraw** 撤回，收回
**例句2:** Troops would be withdrawn.
部队将被撤回。
**withstand** 抵挡，承受
**例句3:** As a politician, he's able to withstand public criticism.
作为一名政治家，他能够承受公众的批评。
**补充笔记1:**
词根 `with-` 在这里意为“向后，反对”。
- `withhold` = hold back (扣住)
- `withdraw` = draw back (拉回)
- `withstand` = stand against (对抗)
